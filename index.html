Mother Cocktails

Here is a list of cocktails any bartender worth their salt should know!

Old Fashioned

Ingredients
  • .25 ounces to .75 ounces of simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostera bitters
  • 2 ounces Bourbon/Rye Whiskey
Process
  1. Put big cube in rocks glass
  2. Add simple, bitters, then liqour to glass
  3. Stir with bar spoon a few times to dilute
  4. Garnish with and express orange peel

Margarita

Ingredients
  • .25 ounces to .75 ounces of agave nectar
  • 1 ounce of lime juice
  • 2 ounces tequila
Process
  1. partial rim your rocks glass with lime then salt or tajin
  2. Add ice, sugar, lime, then tequila in a shaker and shake
  3. Pour contents of shaker in rimmed rocks glass
  4. Garnish with lime wheel or wedge

Tom Collins

Ingredients
  • .5 ounces to .75 ounces of simple syrup
  • 1 ounce of lemon juice
  • 2 ounces of Gin
  • Soda water
Process
  1. Add ice, simple, lemon juice, and gin to collins glass
  2. top with soda water, and stir until uniform

Martini

Ingredients
  • 1 ounce of dry vermouth
  • .5 to 1 ounce of Green Olive Brine
  • 2 ounces of vodka
Process
  1. Add ice, dry vermouth, green olive brine, and vodka to a shaker and shake
  2. Strain ingrdients into martini glass
  3. Garnish with three green olives on a garnish pick

Mojito

Ingredients
  • .5 ounces to .75oz simple syrup
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 2 ounces White Rum
  • Soda water
Process
  1. Add ice, simple, lime juice, and rum into collins glass
  2. Top glass with soda water and stir until uniform
  3. Garnish with expressed mint sprig
