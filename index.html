Traditional Cocktails

Here is a list of cocktails any bartender worth their salt should know!

Old Fashioned

Ingredients
  • Simple = .25oz-.75oz
  • Bitters = 2 dashes Angostera bitters
  • Liqour = 2oz Bourbon/Rye Whiskey
Process
  1. Put big cube in rocks glass
  2. Add simple, bitters, then liqour to glass
  3. Stir with bar spoon a few times to dilute a bit
  4. Garnish with and express orange peel
